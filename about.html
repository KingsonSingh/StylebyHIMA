About StylebyHIMA

StylebyHIMA is a creative direction and styling company founded by Hima Dangwal. We provide styling services that take care of every need of a brand or an individual, from Personal Styling, Commercial, Editorial, Wardrobe, Runway to Visual Merchandising and Product Styling. We believe human mind can never run out of ideas, and as every spark leads us to a theme, stories are captured in photographs. Our aesthetics combine class with modern. Hima Dangwal has a demonstrated history of working in the Fashion industry, also holding a Diploma in Fashion Styling and Image Design. Skilled in Styling, Creative Direction, and Business, she has worked in television and film industries, before starting out as an entrepreneur. In a span of eight months StylebyHIMA worked on a variety of projects with a good number of National and International organisations like Toabh Management Pvt Ltd, The Wadhwa Group, Anima Creative Management and many more.​ StylebyHIMA has also seen an active engagement from international brands like G-Shock, W.Alternative Models(Brazil), MyGuys Models(Milan, Italy), Fashion Modeling Profile(Londrina, Brazil).

About Fashion Careers

It is quite often that students ask us several questions on how to get started in the Fashion industry. While interviewing many, we realised they lack knowledge of the different career paths available for them in the industry. And therefore, StylebyHIMA is launching India's first event 'Fashion Careers.’ The event will be an amalgamation of workshops to be conducted by fashion schools and industry professionals. The event aims to uncover the true depth of the industry and huge variety of roles. To bring together industry leaders, founders and top fashion schools in India to help you explore various professions in Fashion, and to guide you to build your career in the industry. Also at the event we bring companies to offer you internships and sponsorships for you to get a head start.
